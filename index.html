Contact support
How is Nexus different from calling models directly?
+
One contract for forty models, automatic failover when providers go down, semantic caching that cuts cost by up to 70%, and observability that actually tells you what your prompts are doing. You can switch from GPT to Claude to Llama with a config change — no rewrites.
Do you store my prompts or responses?
+
By default we retain logs for 30 days for debugging. Pro and Enterprise plans support zero-retention mode, where requests are processed in-memory and never persisted. We never train on your data.
Can I bring my own API keys?
+
Yes. Bring your own OpenAI, Anthropic, or self-hosted keys and Nexus becomes pure infrastructure — routing, caching, observability — on top of your existing accounts.
What's the migration story?
+
Change one base URL. Our SDK is wire-compatible with OpenAI and Anthropic clients, so existing code works untouched. Most customers migrate in under an hour.
How does billing work for token overages?
+
Pay-as-you-go beyond your plan's included tokens at the model's published rate — no markup. Set hard caps in the dashboard to never get surprised.
Is there a free tier for students or open source?
+
Yes. Verified open-source maintainers and accredited students get 25M free tokens monthly, with access to all frontier models. Apply through the Hobby plan signup.
